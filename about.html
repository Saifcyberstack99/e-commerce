Our Story

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) was founded in Munich, Germany in 1916. Originally an aircraft engine manufacturer, BMW has evolved into one of the world's leading premium automotive and motorcycle manufacturers.

With our headquarters in Munich, we operate 31 production and assembly facilities in 15 countries and have a global sales network in more than 140 countries.
